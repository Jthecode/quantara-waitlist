Privacy Notice

Quantara Technology LLC (“Quantara”, “we”, “us”).

Scope

This notice explains how we handle personal data for the Devnet-0 waitlist, the lightweight Quantara Wallet preview, and the Quantara Faucet (collectively, the “Services”). Testnet tokens have no monetary value.

Data we collect

  • Waitlist form (you provide):
    • Email (required)
    • Role, experience, country/region
    • Discord handle, GitHub profile, referral code (optional)
  • Wallet preview:
    • Runs locally in your browser. Mnemonic/keys are generated client-side and are not sent to our servers.
    • We may log anonymized telemetry (page views, errors) if analytics are enabled.
  • Faucet:
    • SS58=73 address you submit to receive QTR (testnet)
    • Anti-abuse data (IP/network, user-agent, request counts, challenge results)
    • Server logs (timestamps, HTTP metadata)
  • Automatic (all pages): limited cookies or local storage to remember preferences and protect against abuse.

How we use data

  • Operate and secure the waitlist, wallet preview, and faucet
  • Send Devnet-0 updates and guides (you can unsubscribe anytime)
  • Prioritize builders/validators and manage faucet limits
  • Detect, prevent, and investigate spam or abuse
  • Aggregate analytics to improve docs, tooling, and reliability

Legal bases (EEA/UK)

  • Consent — marketing emails; optional analytics
  • Legitimate interests — service security, fraud prevention, basic usage metrics
  • Contract — providing the Services you request (e.g., processing a faucet claim)

Retention

  • Waitlist records: until you unsubscribe or request deletion
  • Faucet logs/anti-abuse signals: typically 30–90 days, longer if needed for security
  • Backups: limited-life, automatically purged on a rolling basis

Security

We use industry-standard controls (encryption in transit, access controls, logging). Keys created in the wallet preview remain in your browser; never share your mnemonic. We will never DM you for a seed phrase.

Vendors & transfers

We rely on reputable processors to run a lightweight stack. Depending on your region, your data may be processed outside your country with appropriate safeguards.

  • Hosting/CDN (e.g., Cloudflare, Vercel, or Netlify)
  • Form handling (e.g., Netlify Forms or equivalent)
  • Anti-bot (e.g., Cloudflare Turnstile or hCaptcha)
  • Email delivery (e.g., Mailgun/Postmark/SES)
  • Optional analytics (privacy-respecting, IP truncation where available)

We’ll keep an up-to-date list of key sub-processors in the repository README or on this page.

Cookies & analytics

We aim to minimize tracking. If analytics are enabled, we use cookieless or low-identifiability modes where possible. Anti-bot challenges may set cookies to prevent abuse.

Your choices & rights

  • Unsubscribe from emails via the link in any message.
  • Access/rectify/delete your personal data where applicable (GDPR/UK GDPR/CCPA/CPRA).
  • Opt-out of sale/share (CPRA): we don’t sell personal information. If you believe otherwise, contact us.
  • Appeal decisions or lodge a complaint with your local data authority.

To exercise rights, email privacy@quantaratech.com with the subject “Data Request”. We may ask you to verify your email.

Children

The Services are for adults and not directed to children under 16. If you believe a minor joined the waitlist, contact us to remove the data.

Changes

We may update this notice to reflect changes to our Services or law. We’ll adjust the “Last updated” date and, when appropriate, provide additional notice.
